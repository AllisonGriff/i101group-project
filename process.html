Author's Note

Thank you for taking the time to learn about our project and read more about Margaret Hamilton. Selecting her as our underrepresented Innovator comes from the lectures for the i101 course. Margaret Hamiliton was always introduced as a key example of inspiring innovators that came from an unrepresented field. As a pioneer in the field of software engineering and women in computer science, Hamilton is a great example of a figure from an unrepresented background whose contributions to computing and representation are still seen today. Our other option would have been Katherine Johnson. Johnson worked at NASA and was vital to the success of NASA first manned space flight. She has also recently received recognition as the main character in the movie ‚ÄúHidden Figures‚Äù. We picked Margaret Hamiliton instead though because of her closeness to computing, the fact that our previous lecture had mentioned her again, and we were first to pick an influential figure.
